About Us


TechWhiz started its business on 5th October 2015 as a trading company that distributes computer hardware and software. We sell numerous hardware components such as printers, CPU, monitors, keyboards and mouse. Other than computer hardware, we also provide direct support for operating systems and software products, such Microsoft Windows 10, Kaspersky Anti-Virus, together with numerous drivers.

Efficiency



We strive to continuously improving internal performance, and provide a very high level of quality and customer service. Everything from inventory allocation, assembly, testing, and quality assurance are done in-house to ensure we provide only the very best for our customers.

Flexibility



Our online store is open for 24 hours, 7 days a week and 365 days. Hence, the availability of our website gives customers the freedom to shop at their own pace and convenience.

Support



We build close, long term relationships with customers based on quality and an in-depth understanding of how to meet their specific requirements. Next, we also transform problems into solutions and we take pride in responding to our clients needs with timeliness, integrity and knowledgeable personal service.
