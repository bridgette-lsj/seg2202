Warranty


This limited warranty shall apply to TechWhiz products. For handset defects under normal use circumstances and at the discretion of the company, TechWhiz shall provide free of charge repair service within the warranty period.

Item Warranty Period Scope of Warranty Warranty Service
Hardware product 1 year Labor and parts Walk in
Peripherals and software 6 months Labor and parts Walk in

The Limited Warranty starts from the day the customer receives the product.


This limited warranty is only valid in Malaysia. Before returning any unit for service, customer should back up data and remove any confidential and/or personal information from the product. TechWhiz is not responsible for damage or loss of any program, data, or removable storage media. Prior to contacting a TechWhiz service agent, please prepare to provide the following information is at hand:

  1. Model, serial number, and IMEI number if available.
  2. Customer's full address and contact information.
  3. Purchase order number, a copy of the customer's original invoice/receipt.

The warranty in respect of a repaired product/part will continue to be warranted for the remaining of its warranty period, or 30 days from the date of completion of repair, whichever is longer. This warranty does not cover the following cases:

  1. The product serial number, IMEI number or warranty seal is illegible or has been removed, erased, defaced, altered, and/or tampered. If any accessory or external part of the product is missing.
  2. Any damage occurs in/on outer surface of the product, including but not limited to cracks, dents or scratches on the exterior cases, screens, camera lenses, buttons and other attachments.
  3. Deterioration of the product caused by normal wearing and tearing, including but not limited to rust or stains.

TechWhiz will determine whether a product is "Out of Warranty" at the company's discretion according to the standards listed above. Repair of Out of Warranty products shall be separately quoted by the TechWhiz service center and respective service shall be provided upon service fee payment.

Shipping



As soon as we've dispatched your order, you'll receive an email from us confirming the details of your purchase and the delivery method you've chosen. We'll point you in the right direction of tracking the progress of your delivery too.


We'll email tracking details of your order to you along with your dispatch confirmation. If you're a registered customer you can also find these details by logging into your account and viewing your order history.


All deliveries will be made via courier service and a signature is needed. Please make sure someone is at the delivery address who will be able to do this. If you do miss your scheduled delivery, the courier should leave a notification card and try again the following working day using the same delivery option. The courier will do this on three consecutive working days unless they're told by you or a Customer Service team member to hold for a future delivery date or that you'll be collecting your gift from the depot instead. If you want to change the delivery address, just let our Customer Helpline team know. If the courier has popped round three times to deliver your goods without any luck, the package will come back to us. If this happens, we'll charge you for any return and ask you to re-send any costs to us.


No need to worry - if part or all of your order is damaged in transit, give our Customer Helpline team a call free on +603 62556655, who are by the phones 24 hours a day, every day and will replace it in a jiffy. In most cases we'll have to collect the damaged goods at an address that's suitable for you. This is for quality control purposes.

Payment


TechWhiz accepts Paypal, MasterCard, VISA, and bank transfer to secure transactions.


After a payment is processed, you will be taken to a confirmation screen that will let you know if your payment was processed successfully or if there was a problem processing your payment. You should also receive an email confirmation with a receipt for your payment. If you are still uncertain that your payment was processed successfully, feel free to call our office to confirm your payment was received.


Payment processing may take 1-2 business days. For example, a payment made after closing time Friday may not be processed until the following Tuesday. To avoid late fees or disruption of service, submit payments at least 2 business days prior to the delinquent date or cutoff date. If this is not possible, we encourage you to make your payment in person to ensure timely credit to your account.


  1. Verify that you have entered all the necessary details when you are trying to complete a purchase.
  2. Please make sure that you bank accounts/credit cards are up-to-date.
  3. If your payment method is set up correctly, but you still cannot complete your purchase, please contact us.
